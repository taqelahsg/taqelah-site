üéØ De-fragmenting AI in Testing

You are in the beginning of 2026. 2025 has been brutal on you in terms of keeping you on the edge of trying to make you learn everything possible about AI. Keynote Speaker

Unmesh Gundecha

Head, Cloud Platform Engineering, Standard Chartered

üéØ The Next-Gen Tester: Navigating the Era of Artificial Intelligence

As AI becomes embedded across software development, testers are no longer grappling with what the technology can do, but with how their role should evolve alongside it. Code, tests, and workflows can now be generated at scale, making execution faster and cheaper than ever. Yet higher automation has not automatically translated into higher confidence. The real challenge has shifted from running tests to interpreting signals, framing risk, and deciding what truly matters.

This talk introduces a practical operating model for testers in the AI era, clarifying which responsibilities must remain human-owned, where AI can effectively amplify thinking, and what work should be fully delegated. Rather than focusing on tools or trends, it helps experienced testers re-anchor their value around judgment, intent, and decision-making. In an environment where AI is widely available, sustained relevance comes from applying it deliberately‚Äîand knowing where human accountability cannot be automated.

Author of "Selenium Testing Tools Cookbook" and leading expert in test automation and quality engineering with global impact.

Currently leads Cloud Platform Engineering at Standard Chartered, helping organizations implement effective test automation strategies and build high-performing QA teams for the AI era.

Performance Testing via Simulation

Traditional performance testing often feels like a brute-force attack: We spin up expensive infrastructure, write complex load scripts (k6/JMeter), and hammer the system until it breaks. This process is reactive, costly, and frequently happens too late.

In this lightning talk, I will demonstrate a radical approach to shift-left testing using Discrete Event Simulation.
